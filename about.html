About

How video generators preserve state and respond to intervention.

CausalVideoGroup studies how video generators maintain scene state over long horizons and how their outputs change in response to control signals or edits.

What this site contains

The project archive presents public research questions, methods, evidence, and limitations. The discussion archive contains the background material prepared before each meeting and the technical conclusions recorded afterward.

Why discussions are public

Paper summaries are prepared before the meeting so that meeting time can be used to examine assumptions, compare explanations, identify failure cases, and design experiments. Only material approved for public release appears here; unpublished plans and submission-sensitive results remain in private group storage.

Core principle: every discussion should identify a central claim, a hidden assumption, a failure boundary, a connection to our research, and a concrete experiment.
