About

Researching causal structure in video.

CausalVideoGroup is a video generation research group focused on causality, controllability, editing, temporal consistency, and evaluation.

What this site contains

The project archive presents public research outputs. The discussion archive records weekly topic summaries, idea maps, key references, critiques, and the intellectual outcomes of group meetings.

Why discussions are public

Our meetings aim to create knowledge rather than repeat paper summaries. Public entries emphasize established research context and insights that can be responsibly shared. Unpublished plans, private results, and submission-sensitive ideas remain in private group storage.

Core principle: every discussion should identify a central claim, a hidden assumption, a failure boundary, a connection to our research, and a concrete experiment.
